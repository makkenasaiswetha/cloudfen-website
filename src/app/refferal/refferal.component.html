Earn Bonus for Consultant and Client Referrals

CloudFen is a great place to work, and we want you to have every opportunity to share our success. Earn a bonus when you refer a friend or associate who becomes a CloudFen consultant, or provide a business lead that results in staffing assignments.

Client Referral Program*

CloudFen pays bonuses for information on new projects and clients. Provide the following information to register project referrals with CloudFen:

  • Company name and location.
  • Project manager or project in need of support.
  • Technical details on the project (an introduction to the project manager increase your opportunity for reward).
